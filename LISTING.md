## App details / Full description
Trinetra SEO is an all-in-one SEO toolkit that helps your Shopify store rank
higher on Google and get cited in AI search. Fix the SEO issues that hold your
store back — in bulk, in minutes.

**What you can do:**

• Meta editor — Bulk-edit SEO titles and meta descriptions across all products
using smart templates, with a live Google search preview and length guidance.

• Structured data (JSON-LD) — Turn on Product, Organization, WebSite,
Breadcrumb, Article, and FAQ schema with a single theme toggle. Become eligible
for rich results like price, ratings, and breadcrumbs.

• Image alt text — Instantly find images missing alt text and auto-fill them
from a template, so you capture Google Images traffic and improve accessibility.

• SEO health score — Get a store-wide SEO score and a prioritized list of the
fixes that matter most, based on your real products.

• Redirect manager — Create and manage 301 redirects to recover SEO value from
removed or renamed pages and eliminate broken links.

No coding required. Trinetra SEO reads your products through Shopify's official
API and never touches your customers' data.

## Key benefits (3, ~80 chars each)
1. Rank higher — fix titles, meta descriptions, and schema in bulk, fast.
2. Win rich results — one-click JSON-LD structured data for your storefront.
3. Recover lost traffic — find missing alt text and fix broken links with 301s.
